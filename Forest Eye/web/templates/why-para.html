Why Did We Choose Pará for Our Study of Deforestation?

From 2002 to 2023, Pará lost 10.7 Mha of humid primary forest, making up 65% of its total tree cover loss in the same time period. Total area of humid primary forest in Pará decreased by 12% in this time period.

From 2001 to 2023, Pará lost 17.0 Mha of tree cover, equivalent to a 16% decrease in tree cover since 2000.

From 2013 to 2023, 99% of tree cover loss in Pará occurred within natural forest.
